About Me

whoami

I tend to describe myself in the following ways:

Father, husband, technologist, casual philosopher, progressive, security enthusiast, eternal student, and servant leader.

Why this site?

Really, just to have a space to express myself. A home away from home. My own little corner of the Internet.

What are my goals? What is my personal mission statement?

I want to learn. I want to grow. I want to discover and become the best version of myself.

I want to have an impact. I want to leave this place a little better than I found it.

I want my work to speak for me in ways that show I care about present and future outcomes, and that I take pride in my results. That I understood and considered deeply.

I want to continue breaking the generational curses that plagued me, and protect my children from them. Then, guide them in preserving these lessons for future generations.

Following this, I want to spread love and be a source for peace. Nothing is more precious than peace, I believe. I want to share any valuable knowledge and experience that I've accumulated with the world.
