- Data science, simulation, data gathering, feature engineering, etc. 
- Game design and general OOP program architecture

I get to study for free. Sure, I could go take a class or a course on any of these topics, *OR* I can dive right in and examine and experiment first hand with an entire Internets-worth of these concepts being manifest right before me.
